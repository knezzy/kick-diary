Kick Diary Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

Kick Diary does not collect, sell, or broker personal data for its own analytics, advertising, or third-party marketing purposes.

Kick Diary is designed to help families track baby kicks, meals, sessions, and related insights. This privacy policy explains what information the app stores and how it is used.

Information you add to the app

Kick Diary stores the information you choose to log, which may include kick timestamps, meal timestamps, session data, diary profile names, and optional profile images.

iCloud sync and sharing

If you turn on iCloud Sync, your diary data may be stored in your iCloud account so it can sync across your devices. If you choose to share a diary, the people you invite can access the shared diary based on the permissions you provide.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Kick Diary does not store your full payment card information.

Exports

If you export CSV files, the exported data is generated from the diary you selected and is shared only when you choose to send or save those files.

Analytics and diagnostics

Kick Diary may calculate insights and summaries from the information you log in order to present charts, comparisons, and movement trends inside the app.

Your choices

  • You can choose whether or not to enable iCloud Sync.
  • You can choose whether or not to share a diary with other people.
  • You can delete logged items and exported files when you no longer want them.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact diarykick@gmail.com
